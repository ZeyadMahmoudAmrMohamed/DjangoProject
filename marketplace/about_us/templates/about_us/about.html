Who We Are

Born From a Love of Exceptional Coffee

Bean & Brew was founded in 2018 by two friends who couldn't stop talking about coffee. What began as weekend trips to farmers' markets became a full-fledged mission: bring the world's finest specialty beans directly to coffee lovers, with zero compromise.

We work directly with growers in Ethiopia, Colombia, Kenya, Guatemala, Indonesia, and Brazil — paying above fair-trade prices because quality and fairness are not separate ideas.

Every batch is small. Every roast is intentional. Every bag arrives within days of roasting.
